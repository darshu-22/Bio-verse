About BioVerse X

What is BioVerse X?

BioVerse X bridges the gap between static textbook diagrams and physical organ models. By utilizing computer vision, it enables touchless, gesture-driven spatial interaction with sub-millimeter accurate 3D organ structures, allowing medical professionals, educators, and researchers to dissect, analyze, and learn with intuitive hand commands.
